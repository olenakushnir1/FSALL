The Origin

The ocean and sea are the most abundant sources for anti-aging bioactive substances. Ascophyllum Nodosum and Gigartina seaweeds from Atlantic Ocean are enriched with polysaccharides (long – chain carbohydrate) proven to enhance skin renewal and skin clarity. Laminaria seaweeds of Iroise Sea are especially rich in highly sulfated polysaccharides that softens fine lines and wrinkles. These are the unique sources to be further refined.

The Refine

Harnessing the power of the uniquely – designed extraction, fermentation and hydrolytic processes,
3 bioactive ingredients are refined from the sustainable managed seaweeds in Atlantic Ocean and Iroise Sea. They are one-of-a-kind extracts enriched with oligosaccharides , highly sulfated oligo-fucoidans and HMW fucoidan complexes. These are the miracle molecules that enhance skin renewal, target pigmentation and stimulate collagen production.
