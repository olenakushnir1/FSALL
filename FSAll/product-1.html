Brightum Technology

Brightum, a unique bioactive complex that unleashes the ultimate resilient power of marine saccharides derived from three unique seaweeds. Ascophyllum Nodosum, Gigartina Stellata and Laminaria harvested from the pristine waters of the Atlantic Ocean are meticulously refined to formulate the core essence of Brightum.

Brightum is a rich source of hydrolytic oligosaccharides, high molecular weight fucoidan complexes, and highly sulfated oligo-fucoidans. This trio of skin bioactives helps to tagret multiple signs of skin aging*.

Directions How to use

1. Unfold the mask and peel off the protective layer.

2. Place mask onto a clean and dry face. Gently massage to smooth out air pockets.

3. Leave on for 10-15 minutes.

4. Remove and discard mask and massage remaining treatment into skin. For maximum benefits, do not rinse off.
